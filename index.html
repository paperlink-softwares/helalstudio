About Me

A Little Intro

Helal has been doing Watercolor and Oil painting since 1982. He likes to paint beauty of nature. He works in various subjects such as landscape, flower, portrait, figures etc. also likes plein air painting and live model portrait painting as well. He specializes in Watercolor and Oil Painting.
He obtained his Masters and Bachelor degrees from Central Academy of Fine Arts, Diploma in Animation from Beijing Film Academy. Both institutions are located in Beijing, China. Also done 3D Animation Graduate Certificate from Seneca College, Canada
